Rules
  1. You need a field to create a sheep.
  2. For sheep to mate, you need to have female and male sheep in one row.
  3. Mating has a 50% chance of creating a lamb.
  4. Click sheep to brand or use the "BRAND RANDOM" button.
  5. Branded sheep will not mate with anybody.
  6. Lamb cannot be branded.
  7. Lamb will randomly become female or male sheep when growing up.
  8. You can't have 2 fields with the same name, but it doesn't apply to sheep.
  9. Mating takes 5 seconds.
  10. Break between mating process takes 8 seconds.
  11. It will take 12 seconds for a lamb to grow up.
  12. Don't leave your browser for too long because sheep will take over your CPU and RAM.
